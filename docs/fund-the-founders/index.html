7 Reforms. No New Money. New Rules.

 
01

Angel Tax Incentive (PAITI)

40-50% deduction on investments up to EUR 200K/year. Modeled on UK SEIS.

Ministry of Finance
02

Pre-Seed Matching Fund

Government co-investment for rounds up to EUR 500K. Singapore model.

Banco de Fomento
03

Portugal Tech Fund II

Dedicated VC fund-of-funds. Track record required. 20-year horizon.

Banco de Fomento / EIF
04

Pension/Insurance Pathway

Enable 1-2% portfolio allocation to domestic VC.

ASF / Ministry of Finance
05

Startup Fund Light

Simplified CMVM vehicle. Reform SIFIDE for full lifecycle.

CMVM / ANI
06

Portugal Capital Bridge

Investor delegations, regulatory reciprocity, CMVM English concierge.

AICEP / CMVM
07

Corporate Acquisition Incentives

SIFIDE-like M&A deductions. Unlock the exit flywheel.

Ministry of Finance
